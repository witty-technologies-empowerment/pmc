Our Propositions!

"Presently people queue in the banks and ATMs over long period of time to withdraw their own money, and most times it comes with a lot of stress because there may not be available cash to withdraw. Thus we are providing a platform for mobile cash request delivery. Just like an UBER where you can book for a ride and the ride comes to you, here at PassMeCash you can request for money and the amount requested will be brought to you without stress and in minutes. "PassMeCash" is a financial service that provides easy accessible funds where ever and whenever it is requested, through fast mobile delivery by connecting our closest agents to the users/clients".
