Our Propositions!

"Presently people queue at the Banks and ATMs over long period of time to withdraw their own money, and most times it comes with a lot of stress because there may not be available cash to withdraw. Thus we are providing a platform for mobile cash request delivery. Here at PassMeCash you can request for money and the amount requested will be brought to you without stress and in minutes. "PassMeCash" is a financial service that provides easy accessible funds where ever and whenever it is requested, through fast mobile delivery by connecting our closest agents to the users/clients".
